Business Requirements Document (BRD)
Project: Dealer Mobile App – Lead Capture Module
Client: Automotive OEM (Confidential)
Version: 1.0
Date: March 2026

1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
The client requires a mobile application for their dealer network to capture, manage, and track sales leads in the field. Currently, dealers rely on manual paper-based or Excel-based lead entry which results in data loss, delayed follow-ups, and poor CRM integration. The new Mobility App will streamline lead capture at dealership touchpoints.

2. BUSINESS OBJECTIVES
- Digitize lead capture process across 500+ dealer locations
- Reduce lead response time from 48 hours to under 4 hours
- Improve lead-to-conversion rate by 20%
- Enable real-time sync with central Siebel CRM
- Provide dealers with offline-capable mobile access

3. SCOPE
In Scope:
- New lead creation (walk-in, phone, referral, event)
- Lead detail capture: name, contact, vehicle interest, budget, source
- Lead status tracking: New > Contacted > Test Drive > Negotiation > Won/Lost
- Follow-up reminder and notification system
- CRM sync (Siebel CRM integration via REST API)
- Dealer-wise dashboard and lead summary
- Manager view with team performance metrics
- Offline mode with sync on connectivity restore

Out of Scope:
- Payment processing
- Vehicle inventory management
- Customer service / after-sales module

4. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Lead Capture
- FR01: Dealer must be able to create a new lead with mandatory fields: customer name, phone number, vehicle category of interest
- FR02: System shall support lead source tagging: Walk-in, Outbound Call, Digital Campaign, Event, Referral
- FR03: Lead form must support photo capture for business cards (OCR optional in phase 2)
- FR04: Dealer must be able to attach notes and voice memos to a lead

4.2 Lead Management
- FR05: Leads must display in a sortable, filterable list view
- FR06: System shall support lead status pipeline view (Kanban-style)
- FR07: Dealer must be able to reassign leads to colleagues
- FR08: Duplicate lead detection based on phone number

4.3 Follow-up & Notifications
- FR09: System shall allow dealers to set follow-up dates with reminder push notifications
- FR10: Overdue follow-ups must be highlighted in the lead list
- FR11: Manager shall receive daily summary notification of team lead activity

4.4 CRM Integration
- FR12: All leads must sync to Siebel CRM within 5 minutes of creation
- FR13: Lead updates in CRM must reflect back in mobile app within 15 minutes
- FR14: Sync conflict resolution: mobile app data takes precedence for field updates

4.5 Reporting
- FR15: Dealer dashboard showing: total leads, conversion rate, avg response time
- FR16: Manager dashboard with team-wise breakdown
- FR17: Export to Excel/PDF

5. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
- NFR01: App must support Android 10+ and iOS 14+
- NFR02: Offline mode must support up to 72 hours without connectivity
- NFR03: App load time < 3 seconds on 4G network
- NFR04: Data encrypted at rest and in transit (AES-256, TLS 1.2+)
- NFR05: Support 10,000 concurrent dealer users

6. TIMELINE
- Phase 1 (Lead Capture + CRM Sync): Q2 2026 (June delivery)
- Phase 2 (OCR, Advanced Analytics): Q3 2026

7. STAKEHOLDERS
- Business Owner: VP Sales, Automotive OEM
- Primary Users: Field Dealers, Sales Managers
- IT: CRM Team (Siebel), Mobile Platform Team
- Vendor: TaTa Technologies (Solution Provider)

8. ASSUMPTIONS
- Siebel CRM APIs will be made available by client IT within 2 weeks of project kickoff
- Client will provide test device pool (Android + iOS) for UAT
- Dealer onboarding and training handled by client internal team
